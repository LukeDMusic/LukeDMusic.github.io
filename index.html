About Luke

          Luke Dougherty is a graduate from Lebanon Valley College in Annville Pennsylvania with a B.A. in Music (with a focus in composition and piano) and a B.S. in Digital Communications. While proficient in most instruments within a band/orchestra setting, he excels in the piano and soprano clarinet. Luke will be teaching English in Japan this coming spring. He is currently the Social Chair in Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia.
